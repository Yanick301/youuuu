## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure user registration, login, and profile management for personalized shopping experiences.
- Product Catalog: Display a curated selection of clothing and accessories for men and women, with detailed descriptions, pricing, and image placeholders.
- Shopping Cart: Allow users to add products to a shopping cart, review items, and proceed to checkout.
- Order Placement & Tracking: Enable users to place orders, track shipping status, and view order history.
- Favorites: Customers may save products to a list of favorites, to view or purchase later.
- AI Outfit Recommender: Suggest products based on browsing history and style preferences, offering personalized recommendations.
- Reviews and Testimonials: Enable users to submit reviews and display testimonials to build trust and credibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#4B0082) to convey sophistication and luxury.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F0F0F0), near white, providing a clean and modern canvas.
- Accent color: Muted Gold (#B8860B) for highlights and call-to-action elements.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for elegant headlines and titles; body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for readability.
- Minimalist line icons for navigation and product features, with a focus on clarity and elegance.
- Clean, grid-based layout with ample whitespace to highlight product imagery and descriptions.
- Subtle transition animations for page navigation and product interactions to enhance the user experience.
